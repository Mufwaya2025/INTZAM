Simple guidance before you begin your application.

Use this page to help borrowers understand what is commonly needed before they apply, especially for Zambia's salaried professionals and civil servants.

Important note

Final approval still depends on IntZam's review process, KYC requirements, and the exact loan product selected.

Basic information

  • Valid identification details
  • Reachable phone number and contact information
  • Residential and employment information
  • Any required KYC or supporting documents

For salaried borrowers

  • Consistent source of income
  • Employer details and role information
  • Repayment affordability aligned with income
  • Clear communication during verification

For business applicants

  • Business purpose and funding need
  • Income or cashflow context
  • Supporting operating details where required
  • Product-fit and repayment suitability

Civil servant focus

For public sector employees, the site now gives a clearer path from first enquiry through application and repayment understanding.

This is useful for ministry staff, council employees, teachers, nurses, and other salaried professionals seeking a more respectful borrowing experience.
